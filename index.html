Practice

How I like to work inside complex systems: a handful of principles scribbled on index cards near the lamp.

Story-first, always

Start with the story learners will tell themselves afterward. Strategy, governance, and design follow from there—not the other way around.

See the whole system

Pair lived experience, qualitative insight, and quantitative data to understand not just what is happening, but why it keeps happening.

Prototype with care

Pilot like you mean it: clear hypotheses, gentle guardrails, generous feedback loops, and a plan for what happens if it works.

Make it survivable

Design practices and processes that honor staff capacity and well-being, not just institutional appetite. Sustainable is a design choice.
