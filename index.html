How I Work

Underneath every project is a way of working: how decisions are made, how partners are supported, and how evidence is used.

What this shows: leadership style, decision-making approach, and how I show up on teams.

I treat every initiative as a learning system—a structure of goals, roles, and feedback loops that can be made visible and improved over time.

The work is to align strategy, operations, and culture so people can do their best thinking together.

  • Systems lens
  • Structured sense-making
  • Comfort with ambiguity
  • Attention to people & pace

Principles I work from

  • Design the system, not just the event or grant.
  • Use evidence to meaningfully shape decisions, not only reports.
  • Hold equity as a core design constraint.
  • Make roles, processes, and expectations explicit.
  • Build in reflection alongside delivery.

How teams tend to experience me

  • Calm, steady presence in complex and time-bound work.
  • Bridge-builder across scientists, administrators, educators, and partners.
  • Clear, structured communicator in messy spaces.
  • Direct, kind feedback grounded in shared goals.
