Selected Signature Work

A few projects that illustrate how I braid research, strategy, and design thinking to shape learning environments and public engagement.

NASA Universe of Learning — National Learning Ecosystem Strategy
Strategic Lead · 100+ institutions · Universities, MSIs, museums & libraries

Through-line: Design a coherent, research-driven learning ecosystem across a highly diverse national network.

What I did:

  • Led mixed-method research: interviews, workshops, surveys, observational studies, environmental scans.
  • Facilitated multi-stakeholder sessions with faculty, institutional leaders, educators, and community partners.
  • Mapped learner journeys & space-use patterns across physical, digital, and community environments.
  • Translated findings into program requirements, partnership models, and high-level spatial & experience implications.

Impact: Created frameworks now used by NASA and partners to guide program design, staffing, and engagement strategies rooted in equity and evidence.

Webb First Images — National Activation & Experience Design
Lead Strategist & Coordinator · 100+ activation sites

Through-line: Turn a once-in-a-generation science moment into a coordinated, locally resonant learning experience.

What I did:

  • Coordinated timelines, evaluation, and communication across more than 100 institutions.
  • Developed adaptable program frameworks and experience outlines partners could tailor to their spaces and communities.
  • Integrated evaluation data into real-time adjustments and post-event learning for future large-scale activations.

Impact: Enabled cohesive, meaningful public experiences across the network and earned recognition within NASA for community activation and strategic design.

Informal Learning Network & Exoplanet Year 10 Pilot
Portfolio Design · Multi-year experimentation

Through-line: Refresh and realign a national informal learning network to better support equity, experimentation, and staff well-being.

What I did:

  • Led strategic reviews of network structure, incentives, and learning agendas.
  • Co-designed pilots testing new engagement models, stipends, and partnership structures.
  • Worked with evaluators to turn feedback into concrete design decisions for future cycles.
Museum-Based Learning Labs & Family Programs
Allentown Art Museum · Worcester Art Museum

Through-line: Build spaces where families and communities can engage with art, story, and each other in ways that feel culturally grounded and welcoming.

What I did:

  • Designed learning labs, intergenerational programs, and bilingual engagement experiences.
  • Conducted visitor studies and observational research to refine spatial layouts and program design.
  • Collaborated with curatorial and education teams to align experience, story, and space.
