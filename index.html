Selected Signature Work

A sampling of projects that show how I braid research, strategy, and design thinking to shape learning environments and public engagement.

NASA Universe of Learning — National Learning Ecosystem Strategy

Strategic Lead · 100+ institutions · universities, MSIs, museums & libraries

Through-line: Build a coherent, research-driven STEM learning ecosystem across a diverse national network.

What I did:

  • Designed and led mixed-method research: interviews, workshops, surveys, observational studies, and environmental scans.
  • Facilitated multi-stakeholder sessions with faculty, institutional leaders, educators, and community partners.
  • Mapped learner journeys and space-use patterns across physical, digital, and community environments.
  • Translated findings into program requirements, partnership models, and experience implications for future initiatives.

Impact: Produced frameworks now used by NASA and partners to guide program design, staffing, and engagement strategies rooted in equity and evidence.

Webb First Images — National Activation & Experience Design

Lead Strategist & Coordinator · 100+ activation sites

Through-line: Turn a once-in-a-generation science moment into a coordinated, locally resonant learning experience.

What I did:

  • Coordinated timelines, evaluation, and communication across more than 100 institutions.
  • Developed adaptable program frameworks partners could tailor to their spaces and communities.
  • Integrated evaluation data into real-time adjustments and post-event learning for future large-scale activations.

Impact: Enabled cohesive, meaningful public experiences across the network and earned recognition within NASA for community activation and strategic design.

Informal Learning Network & Exoplanet Year 10 Pilot

Portfolio design · multi-year experimentation

Through-line: Refresh a national informal learning network to better support equity, experimentation, and staff well-being.

What I did:

  • Led strategic reviews of network structure, incentives, and learning agendas.
  • Co-designed pilots testing new engagement models, stipends, and partnership structures.
  • Worked with evaluators to transform feedback into concrete design decisions for future cycles.

Museum-Based Learning Labs & Family Programs
